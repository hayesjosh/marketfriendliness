Welcome to the:

Housing Market Predictor and Assessment Tool

  • Inspired by the interactive visualizations that allow you to see the past income differences across region, this project allows you to visually assess the future competitiveness of housing markets.
  • Comparisons can then be made against these predicted values based on the user's specific values for FICO (credit) score and specific area of interest.
  • The data that powers this application comes from 26-Million anonymized Freddie Mac home loans between 1991-2018.
  • Samples of these home loans are aggregated across regions, and future values are estimated based upon a time series analysis employing region-specific ARIMA models.
