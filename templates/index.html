Welcome to the:

Housing Market Friendliness Comparison Tool

This project allows you to assess your future competitiveness across housing markets.

Comparisons are made based on the user's specific values for FICO score and area of interest.

Estimations are based off of anonymized Freddie Mac home loans from 1991-2018.

Home loans are aggregated across regions, and future values are estimated by a supervised learning model.
