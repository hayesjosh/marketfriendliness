Welcome to the:

Housing Market Friendliness Comparison Tool

  • This project allows you to assess your future competitiveness across housing markets.
  • Comparisons are made based on the user's specific values for FICO score and area of interest.
  • Estimations are based off of 28-Million anonymized Freddie Mac home loans from 1991-2018.

  • Home loans are aggregated across regions, and future values are estimated using a supervised learning model on time-series features.
